Master Athlete Recipe Database

  1. The Whey Tomato Paste Technique

    To prevent clumping or curdling in hot sauces, mix 30 g (1 scoop) of unflavored whey powder with 2 tablespoons of cold water or almond milk to create a thick, smooth paste. Gradually stir this paste into your tomato sauce over low heat only after the sauce is warm.

  2. Savory Tuna Marinara

    Ingredients: 100 g dry pasta, 150 g tomato sauce, 1 can solid tuna (drained), 30 g whey powder made into a paste. Method: Boil pasta. Heat sauce on low. Stir in whey paste and tuna. Toss with pasta and top with spinach.

  3. Master Recovery Frittata

    Ingredients: 4 eggs, 30 g cheese, 100 g spinach, 50 g cherry tomatoes, 150 g potato cubed. Method: Saut√©e potatoes until golden. Add tomatoes and spinach until wilted. Whisk eggs and pour over mixture. Top with cheese and cook on medium low until set.

  4. Toning Whey Pudding

    Ingredients: 45 g whey isolate, 150 ml unsweetened almond milk. Method: Whisk whey into milk until a thick pudding consistency. Use as a high protein low carb pre sleep bolus to prevent nocturnal muscle breakdown.
