Master Athlete Recipe Database

  1. Whey Tomato Paste Technique

    Make a paste from 30 g whey with 2 tablespoons cold water or almond milk. Stir into warm tomato sauce on low heat to avoid clumping.

  2. Savory Tuna Marinara

    100 g dry pasta, 150 g tomato sauce, 1 can drained tuna, 30 g whey paste. Boil pasta, warm sauce, stir in whey paste and tuna, toss with pasta and top with spinach.

  3. Master Recovery Frittata

    4 eggs, 30 g cheese, 100 g spinach, 50 g cherry tomatoes, 150 g cubed potato. Saut√©e potato, add veg, pour whisked eggs, top with cheese and cook low until set.

  4. Toning Whey Pudding

    45 g whey isolate, 150 ml unsweetened almond milk. Whisk until thick. Use as high protein pre sleep bolus.

Whoop Dynamic Autoregulation Guidance

  • Green recovery greater than 67 percent: increase post workout carbs by ~30 g dry pasta or 1 large potato.
  • Red recovery less than 33 percent: reduce training volume by 20 percent and increase evening healthy fats, for example add 20 g extra nuts or peanut butter to evening snack.
